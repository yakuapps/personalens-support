‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

Is my chat data safe and private?
Yes! PersonaLens processes all data locally on your device. Your conversations never leave your phone and are not uploaded to any servers. We don't collect, store, or have access to your personal conversations.
Which messaging apps does PersonaLens support?
Currently, PersonaLens works with WhatsApp chat exports. We're working on adding support for iMessage, Telegram, and other popular messaging platforms in future updates.
How accurate is the personality analysis?
PersonaLens uses scientifically-backed Big Five personality model (OCEAN) combined with advanced natural language processing. While no automated analysis is 100% perfect, our AI provides meaningful insights based on your communication patterns and language use.
What's the difference between free and premium features?
Free version includes basic personality analysis and compatibility scoring. Premium unlocks advanced insights like predictive relationship analysis, unlimited AI character art generation, detailed sentiment trends, and comprehensive topic analysis.
Why does analysis take a long time?
PersonaLens performs complex AI analysis entirely on your device to protect your privacy. Processing time depends on chat length and device performance. Longer conversations (1000+ messages) may take 2-5 minutes to analyze completely.
Can I analyze group chats?
Currently, PersonaLens works best with one-on-one conversations. Group chat analysis is planned for a future update. For now, we recommend exporting individual conversations for the most accurate results.
My WhatsApp export file won't import. What should I do?
Make sure you're exporting "Without Media" and the file is in ZIP format. If you're still having trouble, try a smaller chat first, or contact support with details about the error message you're seeing.

üîßTroubleshooting

App Won't Load Chat File

Common Solutions:
  • Ensure the file is exported as a ZIP (not TXT)
  • Export "Without Media" to reduce file size
  • Try a shorter conversation first
  • Restart the app and try again
  • Check that you have enough storage space

Analysis Gets Stuck

Possible Fixes:
  • Close and reopen the app
  • Ensure your device isn't in low power mode
  • Try analyzing a smaller chat first
  • Free up device memory by closing other apps

Character Art Not Generating

Premium Feature: AI character art generation requires a premium subscription. Make sure you've completed the upgrade process.

App Crashes

If the app crashes:
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • Try importing a smaller chat file
  • Contact support with crash details
